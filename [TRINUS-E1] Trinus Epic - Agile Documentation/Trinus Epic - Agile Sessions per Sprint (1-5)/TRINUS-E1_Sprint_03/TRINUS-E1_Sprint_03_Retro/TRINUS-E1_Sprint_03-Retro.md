## Executive Summary
Sprint 3 marked a pivotal shift from internal milestones to external impact, focusing on demo-readiness and stakeholder engagement. The team delivered all planned increments, validated navigation, and produced stakeholder-ready demo artifacts. This Retrospective captures lessons learned, improvements, and traceability to the Epic and Agile Journey.

---

## What Went Well
- **Demo-Readiness Achieved:** Delivered a stakeholder-ready UI and demo walkthrough, including video artifacts (US-DEMO-4.1)
- **Navigation Validated:** All top-level and submenu pages were validated, with evidence captured for each (US-NAV-1.3)
- **Traceability Maintained:** All priorities and increments were traceable to Epic Acceptance Criteria (US-PRI-3.3)
- **POC Initiated:** Research and proposal for demo automation and video artifact generation completed (US-DEMO-4.4)
- **Stakeholder Engagement:** Demo was presented to directors and stakeholders, with positive feedback and actionable insights

---

## What Could Be Improved
- **Evidence Unification:** Further unify evidence display across all test types for greater clarity (future US-DEMO-4.3)
- **Automation Coverage:** Expand automation to edge cases and dynamic navigation scenarios (future US-NAV-1.5)
- **Demo Automation:** Accelerate POC implementation for automated demo creation and artifact generation (future US-DEMO-4.4)
- **Cross-Team Collaboration:** Increase engagement with cross-functional teams for broader adoption and feedback

---

## Lessons Learned
- **Visibility Drives Value:** Making results clear and traceable increased stakeholder trust and engagement
- **Empiricism in Action:** Adapting to feedback and constraints ensured delivery of valuable increments
- **Continuous Improvement:** Daily standups and open retrospectives supported rapid learning and course correction
- **Traceability is Key:** Mapping all work to Epic, Blocks, and User Stories enabled transparent progress tracking

---

## Action Items for Next Sprint
- Unify evidence display for all test types (US-DEMO-4.3)
- Expand automation coverage to dynamic navigation scenarios (US-NAV-1.5)
- Implement demo automation POC and integrate video artifact generation (US-DEMO-4.4)
- Foster cross-team collaboration and feedback loops
