## Lessons Learned
- **Adaptation:** Team successfully pivoted from blocked Job Filter module to high-value navigation and evidence work.
- **Empiricism:** Decisions were made based on evidence, feedback, and real constraints, supporting Agile maturity.
- **Transparency:** All increments and decisions were documented and traceable to the Epic and Journey.
- **Collaboration:** Cross-functional teamwork enabled rapid delivery and stakeholder engagement.

## Next Steps & Recommendations
- Continue to expand navigation automation in Sprint 3 (US-NAV-1.3).
- Enhance demo-readiness and evidence display for broader stakeholder review (US-DEMO-4.1).
- Maintain and improve traceability and auditability for all increments.
- Apply lessons from evidence retention POC to future sprints and audit requirements.
